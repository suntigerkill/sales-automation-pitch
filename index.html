// The Problem

Conference companies
bleed revenue every day

Your team is buried in WhatsApp messages, repetitive emails, and manual follow-ups — while leads go cold, invoices sit unpaid, and your best people burn out.

⏱
Slow Lead Response
78% of leads go to the first responder. If your team takes 12-18 hours to reply to a sponsor inquiry, you've already lost the deal to a competitor who replied in minutes.
📊
Unorganised Lead Chaos
Sponsor, speaker, and delegate inquiries all mixed in one inbox or WhatsApp group. No one knows who's handling what, and high-value leads get buried under noise.
🔄
Forgotten Follow-Ups
Most sales need 5+ touchpoints. Your team gives up after 1-2. Leads go cold not because they weren't interested — but because no one followed up at the right time.
💸
Unpaid Invoice Leakage
Lakhs in outstanding sponsor and delegate invoices sit unchased each quarter — because your team is too busy selling to also chase payments manually.
🔥
Team Burnout & Attrition
Your best salespeople spend 60-70% of their day on repetitive tasks they hate. The result: burnout, attrition, and you constantly rehiring and retraining from scratch.
📈
No Visibility Into Pipeline
You don't know how many leads came in this week, which are being worked, or what stage each deal is at. Flying blind makes it impossible to improve or scale.
❄
Dead CRM & Cold Database
Hundreds of past inquirers sitting in a spreadsheet doing nothing. People who showed interest — never systematically nurtured. That's lakhs in untapped revenue.
📋
Inconsistent Delegate Onboarding
Confirmed delegates and sponsors receive event info days late or not at all. Poor post-sale experience kills re-bookings — your cheapest revenue source.
👑
No Sponsor Re-booking Strategy
Past sponsors are your warmest leads — but most companies treat them like cold inquiries the next year. Without a structured re-booking flow, you restart from zero every event cycle, wasting months of relationship-building.
